Our Services

Discover our comprehensive range of services designed to meet all your industrial needs.

Engineering, Procurement, Construction, and Installation (EPCI) Services

We deliver end-to-end EPCI solutions for offshore, swamp, and onshore projects, ensuring seamless execution from start to finish.

Specialty Welding and Machining

Our advanced welding and machining services support emergent and scheduled projects for industrial, chemical, and oil & gas facilities.

Steel Fabrication and Repairs

We specialize in fabricating and repairing pressure vessels, crude oil tanks, water tanks, and other critical infrastructure components.

Anti-Corrosion Treatment

Our anti-corrosion services, including sandblasting and painting, help protect equipment against harsh environmental conditions.

Pipeline Services

We provide chemical flushing, pickling, coating, and wrapping services to ensure optimal flow and integrity of pipeline systems.

Confined Space Tank Cleaning

Equipped with specialized tools and personnel, we ensure safe and efficient tank cleaning for maintenance or decommissioning.

Instrumentation & Controls (I&C) and Electrical Services

We provide maintenance, modifications, and installations to ensure smooth operations of your I&C and electrical systems.

Environmental and Industrial Services

Our waste management and oil recovery services help clients achieve sustainability goals and regulatory compliance.

Maintenance and Soft Craft Services

We deliver premier maintenance and construction services to improve safety, productivity, and operational efficiency.

Training and Capacity Building

Empower your workforce with HSE, technical skills, pipeline maintenance, and instrumentation training led by industry experts.
