About Us

ECA Oil and Industry Services Limited is an indigenous technical engineering company with specialty in welding and fabrication, machine shop services, maintenance/repairs of industrial equipment/plants and manpower development programs through apprenticeship trainings. The company began operation 24years ago and was fully incorporated with the Nigeria corporate affairs commission in 2003.

ECAOISLTD specializes in providing top-tier technical engineering services to the oil and gas, constructions, engineering and manufacturing, maritime sectors and a range of skills acquisition and manpower development trainings.

We leverage on our years of experience while adopting new innovations to deliver values and quality to our esteemed customers efficiently.

Our solutions are tailored to meet the diverse needs of the oil and gas, engineering, construction, manufacturing and maritime industries, in a cost- effective manner, with a strong focus on safety.

Our core services include but not limited to:

Reliable Oilfield Services and industrial Solutions

Expertise in Industrial Solutions

Welding and Fabrication

Equipment/Plants Maintenance/Repairs services

Comprehensive hands-on Apprenticeship Training Programs

Oil Tools and Marine equipment supplies, repairs and maintenances

EPCI Services

Our continuity in business over the years were results of commitment and prioritized safety standards, State-of-the-Art technology, dedicated and skilled workforce, timely and efficient execution of projects.

Our team of professionals is dedicated to ensuring the highest standards of quality and operational efficiency.

Whether you need oilfield services, welding and fabrication including pipeline or steel construction related services, a range of machining services and industrial equipment maintenance, ECA Oil and Industry Services Ltd is equipped to handle your needs with unmatched value for money, quality, precision and timely.

ECAOISLTD on-the job apprenticeship training program in welding and fabrication, machining and equipment maintenance/repairs, has been producing qualified and experienced welders, fitters, fabricators, machinist and maintenance technicians across industries till date.
